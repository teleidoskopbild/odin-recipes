Rice


Learn how to make the best Rice

This recipe is super easy, even for dummies like you!

All you need are some basic ingredients and time.

Ingredients
  • 150g rice
  • 300ml water
  • some salt and oil
Steps
  1. Put some oil in a pot
  2. Add the rice and the water
  3. Add the salt
  4. Cook for 20 minutes.
  5. Dont forget to add cheese and other toppings!
