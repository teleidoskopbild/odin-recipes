Pizza


Learn how to make the best Pizza

This recipe is super easy, even for dummies like you!

All you need are some basic ingredients and time.

Ingredients
  • 360g flour
  • 150ml water
  • one cube of yeast
  • some salt, sugar and oil
  • can of tomatos
  • herbs
  • liquid nitrogen
  • other stuff
Steps
  1. Put the flour, water and yeast in a bowl
  2. Add salt, sugar and some oil
  3. Mix it till soft and fluffy
  4. Put something over the bowl and wait until...
  5. Figure it out yourself
  6. Meanwhile cook the tomatos till you have a nice sauce
  7. Dont forget to add salt, herbs and stuff
  8. Drink the liquid nitrogen
  9. Take the dough and....
  10. Figure it out yourself.
  11. Dont forget to add cheese and other toppings!
