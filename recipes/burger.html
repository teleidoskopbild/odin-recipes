Burger


Learn how to make the best Burger

This recipe is super easy, even for dummies like you!

All you need are some basic ingredients and time.

Ingredients
  • 250g minced beef
  • 2 burger buns
  • some salt and pepper
  • ketchup
  • cheese
  • liquid nitrogen
  • other stuff
Steps
  1. Take the meat and make 2 burger patties
  2. Add salt and some pepper
  3. Put the patties on the BBQ grill
  4. You know the drill!
  5. Figure it out yourself
  6. Dont forget to add cheese and other toppings!
